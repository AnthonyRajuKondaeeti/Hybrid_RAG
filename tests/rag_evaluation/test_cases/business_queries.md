## Complex Queries (Multi-step Reasoning & Analysis)

### Query 6: Profitability Analysis
**Question**: "Analyze TechCorp's profitability margins and explain how they compare across different business segments."
**Expected Answer**: "TechCorp achieved a gross profit margin of 66.3% ($321.8M), operating margin of 20.1% ($97.4M), and net margin of 15.1% ($73.2M). By segment, Cloud Services generates 61.5% of revenue ($298.4M), AI Solutions 25.7% ($124.7M), and Traditional Software 12.8% ($62.1M). The high overall margins indicate strong pricing power and operational efficiency, with Cloud Services being the dominant revenue driver."
**Key Facts**: Multiple margin percentages, segment breakdown, profitability analysis
**Answer Type**: Financial analysis
**Complexity**: Complex

### Query 7: Customer Metrics and Business Health
**Question**: "Evaluate TechCorp's customer acquisition and retention performance, and assess what this indicates about business sustainability."
**Expected Answer**: "TechCorp added 2,847 new enterprise clients with a 94.2% retention rate (vs 87% industry benchmark) and $142,000 average contract value (12% quarterly increase). The 68 NPS score (vs 45 industry average) and high retention rate indicate strong customer satisfaction and business sustainability. This customer health suggests predictable recurring revenue and expansion opportunities."
**Key Facts**: Customer acquisition, retention comparison, contract values, sustainability indicators
**Answer Type**: Customer analysis
**Complexity**: Complex

The 2025 strategy to expand into 5 new international markets suggests plans to increase Asia-Pacific and European revenue share, reducing North American dependency."

## Evaluation Criteria

### Simple Query Success Metrics:
- **Accuracy**: Exact financial figures (95%+ target)
- **Currency**: Proper monetary formatting
- **Context**: Relevant timeframe specification
- **Precision**: Decimal accuracy for percentages

### Complex Query Success Metrics:
- **Analysis Depth**: Multi-factor consideration (80%+ target)
- **Business Insight**: Strategic implications identified
- **Data Integration**: Multiple metrics synthesized
- **Forward-Looking**: Future implications discussed
